Support for People Experiencing Violence
Transformative justice imagines a community-based response to sexual and partner violence that supports survivor wellness and self-determination. Rather than treating people as victims in need of external protection via walls and weapons, collective support empowers survivors to exercise their agency. How can we accompany those who experience violence along the path to healing, power, and change?

Community Engagement Against Violence
Transformative justice imagines a response to sexual and partner violence that is community centered and controlled. Ideally, such a response can build community ties that have been broken through violence, and hold survivors and (if possible) persons who cause harm within a web of healthy relationships.
